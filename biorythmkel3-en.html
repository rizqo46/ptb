    • What is Biorythm?

    The Biorythm theory was developed by Wilhelm Fliess. The theory propose that our daily lives are influenced by the rhythmic cycles described in simple mathematics.

    Biorythm in this model uses three cycles: physical , emotion , and intellectual which each has a period of 23 , 28 , and 33 days. Each of these cycles is in sinusoidal function so it will have points in the form of peaks(1),neutral(0)and nadir(-1).

    • How to use the program?

    Use the form below to enter your date of birth. Use the format mm/dd/yyyy, for example for 23 September 1999, enter is 09/23/1999 and then click input.

    There is no entry limit for the program for birth hours. So it is assumed that the user's birth clock is 00.00 or 12.00AM. The program only display two dates closest to the date of birth for each of the three peaks, normal and valley.
